About Wildlife Gallery

Wildlife Gallery is a digital art space dedicated to raising awareness about endangered and at-risk animal species through original illustration and visual storytelling.

Each artwork in the collection is accompanied by the animal's conservation status and a short description — making complex ecological topics approachable and emotionally engaging for everyone.

This project was created as part of the DGL 104 Application Development program at North Island College. It represents the intersection of art, design, and environmental awareness — a portfolio piece built with care and purpose.
